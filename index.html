Zekun Qi

I am a 2nd-year student joint study in Xi’an Jiaotong University & IIIS, Tsinghua University under the supervision of Prof. Andrew C. Yao. I collaborate closely with Prof. Kaisheng Ma, Runpei Dong and Prof. Li Yi.
In 2022, I obtained my bachelor’s degree in Automation from Xi’an Jiaotong University .
I am currently a research intern of Foundation Model Group at Megvii Inc, where I work with Zheng Ge and Xiangyu Zhang .

My research focuses on Generative Modeling, Multi-Modal Representation Learning, and 3D Computer Vision.
